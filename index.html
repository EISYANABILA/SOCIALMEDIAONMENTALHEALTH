Social Media on Mental Health

How Social Media Affects Mental Health

Social media is a big part of our lives. It helps us connect with others but can also make us feel anxious or lonely. Let’s explore its impact on mental health.

The Positive Impact

  • Helps us stay in touch with friends and family.
  • Raises awareness about mental health issues.
  • Offers support groups for people facing similar challenges.

The Negative Impact

  • Encourages unhealthy comparison with others’ highlight reels.
  • Can lead to cyberbullying and hurt self-esteem.
  • Excessive use may result in feelings of isolation and sadness.

Using Social Media for Good

By using social media mindfully, we can turn it into a tool for good:

  • Limit screen time to avoid overuse.
  • Follow accounts that inspire positivity.
  • Take breaks when feeling overwhelmed.
